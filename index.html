    Welcome to Xianfeng’s Homepage!!

    Xianfeng Wu (CSIG student member) is currently pursuing a B.E. in Artificial Intelligence at Jianghan University, China, and have been fortunate to be mentored by Associate Researcher Zhongyuan Lai . He is involved in several research projects, including the National Natural Science Foundation of China. He has published 5 papers and held 7 computer software intellectual property rights. His main research interests include computer vision and pattern recognition.

    My current research interests are in Machine Learning, Computer Graphics and Computer Vision. In particular, I am interested in the applied aspects of 3D Image Processing, Federated Learning,3D reconstruction and Knowledge Distillation, and aim to further investigate intelligent image processing using knowledge distillation techniques. Prior to this, I explored the detection of point cloud data, with a focus on 3D image processing.

    I am currently looking for a 2024fall Ph.D. position, please contact me directly by email me if you think I am in line with the direction of your lab.
